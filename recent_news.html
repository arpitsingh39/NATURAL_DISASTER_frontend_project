    • January 2025 Southern California wildfires

    • Massive Earthquake and Volcanic Activity (Kamchatka Peninsula, Russia, late July 2025)

      • In late July 2025, the Kamchatka Peninsula in Russia experienced a massive earthquake followed by significant volcanic activity. Emergency services were deployed to assist affected communities, and evacuation orders were issued for areas at risk of volcanic eruptions. The situation remained fluid as aftershocks continued to be felt, and authorities closely monitored the volcanoes in the region for any signs of increased activity.
